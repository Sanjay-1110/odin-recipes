Salmon Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Oil
  • Onion Powder
  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Cucumber

Steps

  1. Marinate the salmon with salt and onion powder
  2. Cook the rice
  3. Dice cucumber
  4. Cook the salmon in a pan with oil
  5. Place the salmon on top of cooked rice along with diced cucumber and Serve!
