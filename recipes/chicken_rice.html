Chicken Rice Bowl

Description

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • Ginger (sliced)
  • Scallions (green and white parts, separated)
  • Garlic (cloves)
  • Jasmine rice
  • Chicken broth
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Cucumber (sliced)
  • Chili oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Poach the chicken: Place chicken thighs in a pot with ginger, green onions, and garlic. Cover with water and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through. Set aside and let cool slightly.
  2. Cook the rice:Rinse rice and cook according to package instructions, substituting chicken broth for water. Add a drizzle of sesame oil for extra flavor.
  3. Shred the chicken:Once cool, shred the chicken with two forks.
  4. Assemble the bowls:Divide cooked rice among bowls. Top with shredded chicken, sliced cucumber, and scallions (both white and green parts). Drizzle with soy sauce and sesame oil to taste.
  5. Optional:Add a drop or two of chili oil for a spicy kick.

Tips

  • Don't discard the poaching liquid! Use it to add flavor to the rice or make a simple soup.
  • For a richer flavor, marinate the chicken in soy sauce, ginger, and garlic for 30 minutes before poaching.
  • Feel free to customize your bowl with other toppings like boiled egg, avocado, or pickled vegetables.
